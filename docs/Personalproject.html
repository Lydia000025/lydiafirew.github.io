Digital Transformation of Community Based Health Insurance

According to the Ethiopian Minestry of health Ethiopian Health Insurance Agency was established
as an autonomous federal government organ through Regulation No. 191/2010 with the objective of
implementing health insurance system in the country. The Agency has established 24 branch offices
to implement the health insurance system all over the country.
The Agency is working on implementation of two types of health insurance systems in the country. The
first type of health insurance system is Community Based Health Insurance (CBHI), which comprises the
community engaged in the informal sectors of the economy. The second type of health insurance system is
Social Health Insurance (SHI), which comprises the population engaged in the formal sectors of the economy.

The Community Based Health Insurance is ideally essential system. However, the implementation is not
the implementation has been through troughs and roughs. The different aspects of the tasks involved makes the
system more complicated. The financial aspect has not been yet estabilished leggaly which suffers much more loss
than other sectors. The other sectors include registration and pharmacethucals. Each task is performed by different
governmental bodies.

Communities suffer from the unorganized process of registration, unwelcoming healthcare service for CBHI users in
the health care centers. The different bodies that perform the tasks has no proper and oranized interaction which
makes is unsafe for patients needing immediate help. For patients to be treated the paper work will lag the process.
I believe Data Science and Data Manegment System with its own server allowing the governmental bodies to interact fast
and secured. The amount of money that would be birbed in the pharmacethucal center for instance was 23 million birr by 2020
Better and responsive service can be provided.
